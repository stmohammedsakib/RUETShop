Acceptance of Terms

RUETShop is a campus-based multi-vendor e-commerce platform developed exclusively for students of Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). The Platform operates as a Software Development Project under the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE).

02

Eligibility

To use RUETShop, you must:

  • Be a currently enrolled student, alumni, or faculty member of RUET

Guest access is available for browsing products, but registration is required for purchasing, selling, or accessing personalized features such as wishlist and order history.

Account Registration

When creating an account, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration
  • Maintain the security of your password and account
  • Accept responsibility for all activities that occur under your account
  • Notify the administrators immediately of any unauthorized use of your account

The Platform uses session-based authentication with a 7-day session expiry and rate-limited login (5 attempts with a 15-minute lockout) for security purposes.

04

User Roles & Responsibilities

RUETShop supports three user roles:

🛒

Normal User

Can browse products, add items to cart, place orders, leave reviews, and maintain a wishlist. Normal Users are expected to complete genuine purchases and provide honest feedback.They can also sell products if they want.

🏪

Dedicated Seller

Can list products for sale, manage inventory, set prices, and track orders. Dedicated Sellers must provide accurate product descriptions and maintain fair pricing practices.

🛡️

Admin

Has full system access including user management, product oversight, order management, and system settings. Admin access is restricted to authorized platform developers.

05

Product Listings

Vendors who list products on RUETShop agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful product descriptions, prices, and images
  • Maintain adequate stock levels and update listings promptly
  • Not list prohibited, illegal, or counterfeit items
  • Comply with campus regulations regarding sale of goods
  • Respond to Normal User inquiries and fulfill orders in a timely manner

RUETShop reserves the right to remove any listing that violates these terms or campus policies without prior notice.

06

Orders & Payments

The Platform supports the following payment methods:

  • bKash — Mobile banking payment
  • Nagad — Mobile banking payment
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) — Payment upon receipt of goods

All deliveries are restricted to the RUET campus. Available delivery locations include Hall Gate, Main Gate, Academic Building, Library, Cafeteria, and custom locations. Delivery is typically completed within 24 hours on campus.

Once an order is placed, it may be tracked via the Orders tab in the user Dashboard. Order statuses include: Processing, Confirmed, Shipped, and Delivered.

Privacy & Data

RUETShop stores all data locally in the user's browser using LocalStorage. This means:

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Data is specific to each browser and device
  • Clearing browser data will remove all stored information
  • Passwords are hashed using multi-round obfuscation for security

We are committed to protecting user privacy within the scope of this campus project. No personal information is shared with third parties.

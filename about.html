Our Mission

RUETShop was born from a simple frustration — students at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology had no dedicated, trusted platform to buy or sell within their campus community.

We built a full-featured marketplace that is fast, secure, and tailored to the unique needs of campus life — from engineering supplies and textbooks to lab equipment and daily essentials.

What Makes RUETShop Special

Every feature is thoughtfully crafted for the best campus commerce experience.

👥

3-Role System

Distinct experiences for Normal Users, Dedicated Vendors, and Admins with tailored dashboards and permissions.

🔐

Secure Auth

Rate-limited login (5 attempts / 15-min lockout), hashed passwords, session expiry, and guest mode.

🛍️

Smart Cart

Persistent cart per user, guest cart that merges on login, real-time stock validation, and coupon support.

❤️

Wishlist

Save products for later, move items directly to cart, and track wishlist counts in real-time.

⭐

Reviews & Ratings

Verified Normal Users leave star ratings with comments. Ratings auto-recalculate on submission or deletion.

📊

Admin Panel

Full control over users, products, orders, and system storage. Change roles, delete entries, reset data.

🚀

Vendor Dashboard

Add/edit products, view customer orders, track inventory with low-stock alerts in one clean view.

🌙

Dark Mode

System-preference detection with manual override. Theme preference persists across sessions.

📱

Mobile Responsive

Fully responsive on all screen sizes — phones, tablets, and desktops with touch-friendly interactions.

🔍

Advanced Search & Filter

Search by name, tag, or description. Filter by category, price range, rating, and stock availability.

💳

Checkout Flow

3-step checkout: delivery info → payment (bKash/Nagad/COD) → order confirmation with ID.

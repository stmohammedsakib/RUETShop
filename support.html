Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a vendor?

Register an account and select "Dedicated Seller" during the sign-up process. Once registered, you will have access to the Vendor Dashboard to list your products, manage inventory, and track orders from Normal Users.

What are the delivery options?

Delivery is restricted to within the RUET campus. You can select specific locations like Hall Gate, Main Gate, Academic Building, Library, or Cafeteria during checkout. You can also specify a custom location if needed.

How can I reset my password?

Go to the Login page, click on "Forgot password?", enter your registered email address, and follow the prompts to verify your identity and set a new password.

What payment methods are available?

We support bKash, Nagad, and Cash on Delivery (COD). During checkout, simply select your preferred payment method and follow the instructions to complete your order.

How do I add products as a vendor?

After logging in as a vendor, go to your Dashboard → My Products tab → click "Add Product". Fill in the product name, category, price, stock, and description. You can also add an image URL or emoji.

Can I use the platform without an account?

Yes! You can browse products and add items to your cart as a guest. However, to complete a purchase, leave reviews, or manage a wishlist, you'll need to create an account or sign in.

How do I track my orders?

Go to your Dashboard → Orders tab to view all your placed orders. Each order shows its current status (Processing, Confirmed, Shipped, Delivered) along with order details and delivery information.

Is my data secure on RUETShop?

Yes! RUETShop uses hashed passwords, session management with automatic expiry, and rate-limited login (5 attempts with 15-minute lockout). All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
